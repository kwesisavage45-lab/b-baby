About Us

Who We Are

B-BABY COLLECTIONS is a women's fashion brand dedicated to celebrating every woman's unique style. Founded with a love for quality, comfort, and elegance, we curate pieces that move with you — from morning to evening.

Our Mission

We believe great fashion should be accessible to every woman. Our mission is to bring you thoughtfully designed clothing that fits beautifully and feels even better — at prices that make sense.

Why Choose Us

  • Carefully curated, quality-checked pieces
  • Fast, reliable delivery across Ghana
  • Easy returns within 14 days
  • Dedicated customer support

FAQ's

How long does delivery take?

Orders within Accra are typically delivered within 1-2 business days. Other regions in Ghana usually take 2-4 business days depending on location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money, debit/credit cards via Paystack, and cash on delivery in select areas.

Can I return or exchange an item?

We carefully inspect every order before dispatch, so returns and exchanges are only offered if we made a mistake — for example, you receive the wrong item, a damaged or defective item, or something significantly different from what you ordered. Change of mind, or ordering the wrong size or colour, isn't eligible for return.

What if I receive a damaged, defective, or wrong item?

Contact us on WhatsApp within 48 hours of receiving your order with your order number and clear photos or videos of the issue. We'll review your claim and arrange a refund, replacement, or exchange if the mistake is ours.

How do I track my order?

Open the menu and tap "My Orders" to see the status of every order you've placed with us.

Do you ship outside Ghana?

Currently we deliver nationwide within Ghana only. Reach out to us on WhatsApp if you have a special request.

How do I know my size will fit?

Each product page includes a size guide. If you're unsure, message us on WhatsApp with your measurements and we'll recommend the best fit.
